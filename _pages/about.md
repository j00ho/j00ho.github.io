I am an incoming M.S. student in the [Graduate School of Data Science (GSDS)](https://gsds.kaist.ac.kr/) at [KAIST](https://www.kaist.ac.kr/en/) starting Fall 2026, advised by [Prof. Chanyoung Park](https://dsail.kaist.ac.kr/professor/). I received my B.S. in Mathematics from KAIST, with a minor in the School of Computing and the Entrepreneurship Program.

My research focuses on developing reliable and broadly applicable **neural operator** architectures for physics simulation, grounded in their mathematical foundations and geometric extensions. More broadly, I am interested in **Scientific ML** and **physically-constrained world models** — building efficient, physics-informed surrogate models that generalize across complex systems, and tackling **inverse design problems** where the goal is to find geometries or configurations that achieve target physical properties.

Recently, our paper *EqGINO: Equivariant Geometry-Informed Fourier Neural Operators for 3D PDEs* has been accepted at **ICML 2026**. [[paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.03260)]

#### Research Interests
- Neural Operators for PDE Solving
- Physically-constrained World Models
- Inverse Problems & Design Optimization
